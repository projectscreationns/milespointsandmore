What Should Your First Credit Card Be?

BeginnersCredit Cards

No matter if you are a student or if you are just building credit history in the US, your first credit card should be Discover Card. It's one of the easiest to get approved for.

Get this the moment you get an SSN (for foreigners) and for any Americans who are either in college or just looking to get your first credit card. The sooner you get this card the sooner you can build your credit to go after the rewards cards.

Use the link below to apply and earn $100 statement credit:
Discover Card - $100 statement credit

If for any reason you get denied for a Discover card, then that's mental. Your only hope then is to get a secured card. ANY secured card. Use it for 6 months, pay off the statement balance each month, that means NO interest.
