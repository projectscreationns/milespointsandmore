Deal Alert: 50,000 Qantas Points on the Amex Ultimate — Ends 28 July

DealAustraliaQantasAmex

Quick one for the Aussies. The Qantas American Express Ultimate Card is running a 50,000 bonus Qantas Points offer, and it closes on 28 July 2026. If you've been sitting on the fence, this is your nudge.

The deal

Apply online, get approved, and spend $5,000 in the first 3 months — 50,000 Qantas Points land in your account. That's it. No mystery, no hoops.

50,000 Qantas Points is genuinely useful. That's a one-way business class hop to Asia territory, or a chunk of a bigger redemption if you're pooling. I'd value it well north of the annual fee for anyone who actually flies.

Is it worth it?

If you can hit $5,000 in 3 months without manufacturing spend you don't need — yes. This is a straight sign-up bonus play, which regular readers know is exactly where I think the real value lives. You're not applying for the day-to-day earn rate; you're applying for the 50K and moving on.

Couple of things to check before you click apply:

  • Amex AU rules: you generally can't get the bonus if you've held this card (or gotten the bonus) recently. Check your history first.
  • The annual fee — weigh it against the 50K. For most people who value Qantas Points, it clears easily in year one.
  • Timing: the offer ends 28 July 2026. Approvals aren't always instant, so don't leave it to the last day.
Bottom line: 50,000 Qantas Points for $5K of spend you were going to do anyway is an easy yes — but only if you can meet the spend and you're clear of Amex's re-bonus rules. Grab it before 28 July.

As always — check the current terms on Amex's site before applying, because these offers and their fine print change without much warning.
