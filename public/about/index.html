About

I'm Navees. I churn credit cards, chase points, and fly in seats I could never actually afford to buy.

This site is where I write down what actually works — sign-up bonus strategy, the redemptions that made it all worth it, and the deals I'd tell a mate about. I cover both sides of the world: US cards (Chase, Amex, AA, the whole 5/24 dance) and Australian points, banking and savings.

No fluff, no affiliate-spam nonsense. Just real numbers, honest opinions, and the occasional rant about the stupid rules banks make us play by.

What you'll find here

  • Deal alerts — sign-up bonuses and bank offers worth grabbing, AU and US.
  • Strategy — which card next, how to stay under 5/24, why SUBs beat category cards.
  • Redemptions — how I actually spend the points.
  • Comparisons — Revolut vs Wise, savings accounts, and more.

New posts land roughly once a week. If that sounds useful, drop your email below.
