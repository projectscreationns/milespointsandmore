Best Cards Right Now

First question: what's your game - cashback or travel? Don't try to do both at once. Pick your lane, grab the best card for it, and move on. Here's my current top 5 for each. I keep this page updated - the sign-up bonuses move around, so this is what's actually best right now.

Last updated Jul 9, 2026

💵 Top 5 — Cashback

Chase Ink Business Cash Chase
$1,000 (100,000 UR points)
Spend: $8,000 in 3 months Annual fee: $0

Best offer ever right now and no annual fee. Easiest $1,000 you'll make. You just need a 'business' (a side hustle / sole trader counts).

Chase Ink Business Unlimited Chase
$1,000 (100,000 UR points)
Spend: $8,000 in 3 months Annual fee: $0

Same huge bonus as the Cash, flat 1.5% on everything. Pair it with a Sapphire and those points turn into travel worth way more.

Capital One Savor Capital One
$250 cash
Spend: $500 in 3 months Annual fee: $0

Tiny $500 spend for $250. Lowest-effort win on this list. Great if you don't want to chase a big minimum spend.

Discover it Discover
$200 + all cash back doubled in year one
Spend: Low Annual fee: $0

The beginner card. Cashback Match doubles everything you earn the first year. Easiest approval if you're new to credit.

Chase Freedom Unlimited Chase
Extra 1.5% on everything (up to $20k) year one
Spend: None Annual fee: $0

No annual fee, keeps earning UR forever. A solid keeper card once the bonus cards are done.

100,000 UR points
Spend: $5,000 in 3 months Annual fee: $95

Matches the all-time-high offer. The best travel starter, full stop - transfer points to airlines/hotels for outsized value.

Chase Ink Business Preferred Chase

Capital One Venture Capital One
75,000 miles

Chase IHG Rewards Premier Chase
185,000 points
